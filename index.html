Random Mulitple Choice questions will go in this space.

Each question will have 4 possible answers, with radio buttons.

There will be a submit button. When hit:
if answer is correct, a box will appear proclaiming "YAY! You conquered that question!"
else, "Oops, would you like to"

Try again!
Get a hint!
Move on! - each of these three choices will be a button.

A box will also appear below, telling them their total score.

Penultimately, this site will be organized such that the student is able to choose questions from specific calculus topics, sub-topics, or random.

Ultimately, it will be a game, with different amounts of points given, corresponding to the level of difficulty of the questions.

The purpose of this site is to help high school students practice for the AP Calculus AB and BC exams.
The questions are modeled after those that have appeared on previous exams. (The actual questions are copyrighted by the CollegeBoard.)
